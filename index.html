About Rishiram Bhusal

Welcome to my space on the web! I’m a passionate sports enthusiast who loves cricket and football, with a deep admiration for Lionel Messi. Beyond the field, I have a keen interest in global affairs and astrophysics, often diving into books that explore international relations and the mysteries of the universe.

Academically, I hold an MSc in Physics from Tribhuvan University, which has shaped my analytical thinking and curiosity about the cosmos. Currently, I serve as an employee of the Government of Nepal, contributing my skills and knowledge in my professional field.

Whether it’s the thrill of a last-minute goal, the complexities of diplomacy, or the wonders of space, I find joy in learning and exploring diverse subjects.

Feel free to connect and explore my interests further!
